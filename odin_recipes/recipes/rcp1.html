STRAWBERRY SORBET

Delicious Sorbet for the Hottest Days!

An easy yet tasty strawberry sorbet to make. Only two ingredients and a plate full of happiness!


INGREDIENTS

  • Two cups Frozen Strawberries
  • Â¼ cup Sweetened Condensed Milk

STEPS

  1. Put strawberries and condensed milk in a high-powered blender
  2. Blend, stopping occasionally to scrape down the sides of the blender with a spatula, until completely smooth
  3. Transfer into a freezer container and freeze for 2 hours

Nutrition Facts
Per Serving: 200 calories; protein 4g; carbohydrates 41g; fat 3.6g; cholesterol 13mg; sodium 53m
